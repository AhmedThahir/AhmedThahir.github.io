## $e^z$

$$
\begin{align}
|e^z| &= e^x \\e^{z + (2k \pi) i} &= e^z
\end{align}
$$

## $\log z$

$$
\begin{align}
\log z
&= \ln r + i \theta \\&= \ln r + i[\text{Arg}(z) + 2k\pi] \\
\text{Log} z
&= \ln r + i[\text{Arg}(z)]
\end{align}
$$

## Complex Exponents

$$
\begin{align}
z &= e^{\log z} \\z_c &= e^{c \log z} \\PV(z^c) &= e^{c \text{ Log} z}
\end{align}
$$
