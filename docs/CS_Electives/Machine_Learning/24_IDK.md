# IDK

Deriving the coefficients as a distribution

Limitation: only works for a single parameter
$$
n_\text{eff} = \dfrac{n(n-1)}{2}
$$

## Linear Fit

$$
\begin{aligned}
y &= mx + c \\
\implies m &= \dfrac{y_i-y_j}{x_i - x_j} \sim \text{pdf}(m)
\end{aligned}
$$

## Exponential Curve

$$
\begin{aligned}
c_t &= c_0 e^{kt} \\
k &= \dfrac{-\ln \vert c_i / c_j \vert}{i-j}
\sim \text{pdf}(k)
\end{aligned}
$$
